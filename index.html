About BluePeak

Where Trust Meets Expertise

At BluePeak, we understand that today’s businesses, whether startups, SMEs, established corporates, or professional firms face growing financial, regulatory, and operational challenges. From navigating complex tax laws and evolving Nepal Financial Reporting Standards (NFRS/IFRS) to managing payroll, ensuring accurate financial reporting, and maintaining full regulatory compliance, these responsibilities can quickly become overwhelming. That’s exactly where we step in.

Founded by a qualified Chartered Accountant with a deep passion for excellence and a clear vision of supporting Nepal’s growing business landscape, BluePeak delivers comprehensive financial advisory services tailored to your unique needs. We go beyond traditional accounting to act as your strategic partner, simplifying complexities and providing practical, actionable solutions that drive sustainable growth.

Our Core Services

  • Financial Reporting & Compliance: Expert preparation and review of financial statements in full accordance with NFRS/IFRS, ensuring accuracy, transparency, and audit readiness.
  • Tax Advisory & Planning: Strategic tax compliance, return filing, and optimization for corporate, individual, and VAT requirements under Nepal’s Income Tax Act and regulations.
  • Payroll & HR Compliance: End-to-end payroll processing, statutory deductions (SSF, CIT, etc.), and HR-related compliance to keep you focused on your team rather than paperwork.
  • Business Advisory & Decision Support: Budgeting, forecasting, cash flow management, and insightful financial analysis to help you make informed, confident decisions.
  • Regulatory & Outsourcing Support: Assistance with company registration, annual filings, internal controls, and outsourcing of routine financial tasks for efficiency.
