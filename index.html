NFRS & IFRS Advisory Services

International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) are globally recognized accounting standards set by the International Accounting Standards Board (IASB). In Nepal, the Nepal Financial Reporting Standards (NFRS) have been developed in compliance with IFRS, with phased implementation overseen by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nepal (ICAN) since the fiscal year 2014/15.

At BluePeak, our experienced team of professionals helps clients understand and implement NFRS, including the first-time adoption of these standards. We work closely with management to ensure a smooth transition, addressing all key changes and requirements while providing practical guidance for compliance and accurate financial reporting.

Our NFRS & IFRS services include

  • First-time NFRS/IFRS implementation
  • Advisory on major accounting changes
  • Ongoing compliance support
  • Training and capacity building for finance teams
